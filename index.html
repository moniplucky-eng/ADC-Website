Common questions

Is there a lock-in contract?

No. All ADC packages are month-to-month. We earn your business every month.

Can I upgrade between tiers?

Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time and we'll pro-rate your billing accordingly.

Where is my data hosted?

All data is hosted in Australian data centres — Azure Australia East or AWS ap-southeast-2. It never leaves Australian shores.

What does the free Digital Health Check involve?

We review your current digital setup — website, email, security posture, and IT infrastructure — and give you an honest assessment with a recommended package. No obligation.
